Use Case

CS students preparing for placements today juggle multiple fragmented platforms — coding sites, MCQ banks and aptitude portals — with no unified tracking or placement-specific simulation.

Our platform brings everything into one environment. Students customise a timed test by selecting topics and difficulty, write code in a browser editor that runs in an isolated Docker sandbox, and get instant feedback.

Administrators manage question banks and configure assessments through a dedicated console. The end result: a student opens our platform, practices weak areas, takes a mock test, and walks into their placement interview actually prepared.

Functional
FR-1Students register, log in and manage their profile
FR-2Customise test by topic, question type and difficulty
FR-3System assembles and delivers a timed test
FR-4Code runs in an isolated Docker container
FR-5Results and scores stored and displayed instantly
FR-6Admins add, edit and delete questions
Non-Functional

NFR-4Availability — designed to maintain high uptime
NFR-5Usability — responsive UI, no training needed
